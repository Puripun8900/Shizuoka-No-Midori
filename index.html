About Shizuoka Tea

The Heart of Japanese Tea Culture

Shizuoka Prefecture is the heart of Japanese tea culture, producing over 40% of Japan's tea. Located at the foot of Mount Fuji, our region's unique climate, with its mild temperatures, adequate rainfall, and mountainous terrain, creates the perfect conditions for growing exceptional tea.

Our Heritage

We work directly with local tea farmers who have been perfecting their craft for generations. Many of our partner farms have been family-owned for over 200 years, passing down traditional cultivation and processing techniques from father to son.

From Garden to Cup

Each tea is carefully selected, processed using traditional methods, and packaged fresh to bring you the authentic taste of Shizuoka. From the ceremonial grade matcha used in traditional tea ceremonies to the everyday sencha perfect for daily enjoyment, we offer the finest selection of Japanese teas available.

Our Mission

We believe that great tea brings people together and creates moments of peace in our busy lives. Our mission is to share the authentic taste and cultural heritage of Shizuoka tea with tea lovers around the world.
