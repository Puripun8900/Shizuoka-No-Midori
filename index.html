From Shizuoka with Love

Nestled at the foot of Mount Fuji, Shizuoka prefecture has been Japan's premier tea-growing region for over 800 years. Our family has been cultivating these sacred leaves for four generations, perfecting the art of growing and processing the finest Japanese tea.

Each cup tells a story of tradition, craftsmanship, and the unique terroir that makes Shizuoka tea exceptional. From ceremonial grade matcha to everyday sencha, we bring you the authentic taste of Japan.
